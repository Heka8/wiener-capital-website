Who We Are

Investing in What Matters

Wiener Capital Oy is a Finnish development and investment company with a clear mission: to identify, develop, and scale technologies that address the world's most complex challenges.

We combine deep industry expertise with strategic capital allocation to support ventures that push the boundaries of what is possible. Our portfolio spans across industries and geographies, united by a common thread — meaningful technological advancement.
